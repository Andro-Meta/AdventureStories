    About Adventure Stories

    Adventure Stories is an AI-powered, text-based RPG where your choices shape the narrative, guided by an AI storyteller.

    Create characters, explore themed worlds, battle monsters, find items, and complete your adventure goal! The game uses a local MiniCPM-2B-128k AI model to power the storyteller (no API keys required).

    Features: Multiple themes, dynamic goals, combat, status effects, item tiers, AI-generated choices mapped to fixed action types (Good, Bad, Risky, Silly, Investigative) with implicit consequences, expandable story display, local saves, faction reputation system with trust-based difficulty.

    Made with ❤️ for Brookston, Vincent, Toby, and Katie by their Dad.
